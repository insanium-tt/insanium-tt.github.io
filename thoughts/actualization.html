about now thoughts

I've been a fan of CGP grey a while. He has this video, "your theme", that proposes replacing your new years resolution with a "theme" for the year. Last year, I subscribed to that idea. My theme was "the year of focus," and I would say I was moderately successful.

I have spent the year learning, observing. Although I intend to learn and grow throughout my life, it is time for me to focus on doing more. It is time for me to actualize on my knowledge.

Power

So, if I wish to actualize with the end-goal of accomplishing my ambitions, I think it's implicit I think deeply about how my public perception and how I wield my power. This implicit from the fact that my ambitions inherently require navigating institutional and social structure to some extend. I have to think more deeply about how I communicate, how I'm perceived, etc.

Obviously I shouldn't get super wrapped up in my own public perception. It's unhealthy, as any high schooler would tell you! But, like any part of my life, how I present myself requires deliberation, thought, and curation to maximize effectiveness.

This leaves the question: how do I curate my public image to maximize my power? There are books on this sort of thing, e.g., 48 laws of power. I observe very very closely what makes others powerful and successful. It's time for me to start superimposing what I have learned onto my own life. Of course, I want to be subtle and not fucking stupid with it. Power, in general, is curated extremely in extremely subtle ways.
