Insanium

about now thoughts

Following my breakup in August, I have entered a period of intense self-reflection and a deep loneliness. I haven't felt anything like this before, and I feel the ramifications of this time period will have an intense effect on the rest of my life.

For the past 5 years, in one way or another, I've had someone who I could confide in, someone who I can be close to. Someone I could share all the stupid details of my life with, someone who I can truly be myself around. But now, I have no one. All my thoughts are trapped in my head, with no one to hear them but myself.

During my breakup, I was in another country where I knew very few people. I worked in a lab, so I got to know some of the grad students and professors a little bit. Yet, despite my isolation, I didn't feel so lonely. I felt content on my own, doing my own thing. Perhaps that was temporary and not of general significance, as it was too close to my breakup.

Upon coming back home and re-entering a normal social life, I've felt more lonely than ever. I'm surrounded by people I know, yet I'm close to none of them. I have good friends I deeply trust, but nothing like the relationship with my ex. Being surrounded by less than desirable relationships is a constant reminder of what I once had.

I know so many damn people would be satisfied with my situation. Because, in all honesty, my social life is pretty good. But, of course, I'm not. I've been given a taste of what a truly deep, comprehensive relationship is like. And, now that I don't have something like that anymore, I'm left with a big, gaping hole in my heart that yearns to be filled. Maybe if I knew what I knew now, I wouldn't have let myself fuck up my past relationships. It's not worth crying over spilt milk, I guess.

It's been a couple months since that breakup, and I'm feeling a little better now. Maybe I could try dating again. In all honesty I've had my eye on someone who I want to get to know better. Either way, no matter who I end up with next, I will constantly remind myself of the way I've felt now. Hopefully reminding myself of the deep loneliness I feel now will help me better handle future relationships. Maybe, just maybe, I could make someone happy. To do so, I fucking swear I'll become the best goddamn person I can be.
