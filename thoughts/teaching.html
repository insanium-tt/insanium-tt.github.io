about now thoughts

I've found myself in a position where many different people, many of whom I deeply respect, approach me for academic and personal advice. People which I perceive to be better than me in many different respects look to me of all people for advice on various things. How the fuck? For maybe the first time in my life I am feeling a large amount of imposter syndrome. I understand I do not need to be better than someone at anything to give effective advice; in general, I enjoy helping people and I think I'm at least decent at it. Yet, in a weird emotional way, I struggle to do so.

Those that entrust me with their insecurities and ask me for advice seem to perceive me very highly. I genuinely cannot comprehend how this can be. Okay, I understand this is a product of me keeping my sins hidden from everyone but myself, but understand how conflicting this feels for me. Those seeking my advice have no idea how flawed I truly am. They have no idea of the sins I've committed. They have no idea what a horrible, impure, disgusting person I am.
